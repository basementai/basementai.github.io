About Basement AI

Basement AI is a private AI research lab and consultancy focusing on helping organisations to identify and unlock the value of their data through artificial intelligence. We conduct research on natural language understanding and support both commercial and public sector organizations with their data, AI and natural language processing related initiatives. Our expertise and experience ranges from strategy definition to hands-on development of state-of-the-art machine learning and NLP systems.

People

Aarne Talman

CEO & Head of Consulting

   

Aarne has over 13 years of experience in AI, ranging from academic research to strategy consulting. Prior to founding Basement AI, Aarne led the Analytics consulting practice in the Nordic region at Gartner. He holds a PhD research position at University of Helsinki Language Technology group. Aarne leads Basement AI's consulting business and has the overall responsibility of the management of the company.

Alessandro Raganato

Head of Technology

 

Alessandro holds a PhD in Computer Science from Sapienza University of Rome. His specialities include natural language processing and machine learning -- in particular computational models of natural language meaning. He is also a researcher at the language technology research group at University of Helsinki. Alessandro leads the development of new products and services as well as experimentation with the latest technologies at Basement AI.

Stergios Chatzikyriakidis

Head of Research

 

Stergios holds a PhD in Theoretical Linguistics from King's College London. He specializes in computational semantics and has published a textbook and more than 50 academic papers and book chapters on the topic. He is also an associate professor and the coordinator of CLASP research center at University of Gothenburg. Stergios leads the development and execution of the research agenda of Basement AI.
