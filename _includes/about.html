About Basement AI

Basement is a private AI research lab and consultancy focusing on helping organisations to identify and unlock the value of their data through artificial intelligence. We conduct research on natural language understanding and support both commercial and public sector organizations with their data, AI and natural language processing related initiatives. Our expertise and experience ranges from strategy definition to hands-on development of state-of-the-art machine learning and NLP systems.

Aarne Talman

Founder

     

Aarne has over 13 years of experience in AI, ranging from academic research to strategy consulting. Prior to founding Basement, Aarne led the Analytics consulting practice in the Nordic region at Gartner. He holds a PhD research position at University of Helsinki Language Technology group.
