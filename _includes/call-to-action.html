About Basement AI

Launching in 2019 - Basement AI is a private AI research lab and consultancy focusing on helping organisations to identify and unlock the value of their data assets. We conduct research on natural language understanding and support both commercial and public sector organizations with their data, AI and natural language processing related initiatives. Our expertise and experience ranges from strategy definition to hands-on development of state-of-the-art machine learning and NLP systems.
